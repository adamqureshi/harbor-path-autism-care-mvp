For families

A simpler first step into autism support.

Families should not have to navigate ABA alone. Harbor Path is designed to make the beginning easier: clear intake, practical caregiver coaching, and virtual sessions that fit real home routines.

Get early access
What can virtual support help with?

Routines, caregiver coaching, communication goals, behavior support planning, and follow-through at home.

Is this AI therapy?

No. Harbor Path is clinician-led. AI supports administrative workflows, summaries, and progress tools.

Who is this for first?

New York families looking for a clearer starting point for ABA support, especially on Long Island.

Can I use insurance?

For MVP testing, we are learning family needs and provider availability before opening full payer workflows.

How it works

From first question to first session.

1

Tell us what you need

Complete a simple early-access form. We keep it lightweight and avoid collecting sensitive medical details in public forms.

2

Review fit

Harbor Path reviews location, care goals, availability, and whether virtual ABA support could be appropriate.

3

Match with a clinician

Families are matched with a qualified clinician when the care need, schedule, and licensure fit are aligned.

4

Start support from home

Caregivers get practical coaching, session takeaways, and progress visibility reviewed by the clinician.
